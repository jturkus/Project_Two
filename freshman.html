The Freshman Scholarship is an exclusive opportunity for freshmen to meet the current members of our chapter. After applying, they have the opportunity to win $500 for first place and $250 for second place. Applicants are interviewed by members of the chapter for and the awards are presented at a formal banquet. Scroll down to see photos from the 2014 Freshman Scholarship Banquet.
